Brief Biography

   

I am a M.S. student in Prof. Shengfeng He's team in the School of Computer Science and Engineering, South China University of Technology (SCUT). Before SCUT, I have received the B. Eng. degree from the School of Electrical Engineering and Automation, China University of Mining and Technoloy (CUMT), where I worked on robot control supervised by Prof. Yanzi Miao. Between CUMT and SCUT, I have three years of work experience serving as a R&D Simulation and Control Engineer at China Nuclear Power Technology Research Institute. My current research interests include machine perception, multimodality and theoretical machine learning. More specifically, I am working on the problem of crowd scene analysis and crowd counting. Now I work as a Research Intern dedicated to self-supervised 3D vision in Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, under the supervision of Prof. Dan Xu. Click here to view my up-to-date CV.
